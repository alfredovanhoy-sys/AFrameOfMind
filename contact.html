Planning Your Visit

Booking Information

We recommend booking at least 2-3 weeks in advance, especially during peak seasons (summer and fall). Weekend bookings fill up quickly, so early planning is essential for securing your preferred dates.

What to Bring

Our cabins are fully equipped with linens, towels, and kitchen essentials. We recommend bringing comfortable hiking clothes, weather-appropriate outerwear, and any personal items you'll need for your stay.

Local Activities

The area offers excellent hiking trails, wildlife viewing opportunities, and seasonal activities like berry picking and stargazing. We're happy to provide recommendations based on your interests and fitness level.

Transportation

Our cabins are accessible by car year-round. We provide detailed directions upon booking. The nearest airport is 45 minutes away, and we can arrange transportation if needed.
